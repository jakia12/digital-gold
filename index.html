BitGold — The Bitcoin Predecessor

BitGold was a digital currency and payment system that was created by the Canadian entrepreneur Nick Szabo. It was an early precursor to Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies way back in 2005. BitGold aimed to create a decentralized digital currency that would enable secure online transactions without the need for intermediaries like banks, just like Bitcoin is, or at least should be, today. BitGold never gained widespread adoption or recognition like Bitcoin did later on.
